Apocalypse Bus

The DNDIY mascot and mobile laboratory.

Originally purchased from a church outside of Olympia Washington it has slowly transformed into the vehicle commonly used by DNDIYers today. Among other things it comes equipped with a mobile welder beverage carbonator combo, mood lighting, and an apocalypse survival kit.
