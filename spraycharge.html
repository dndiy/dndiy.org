Refillable Aresol/Spray Paint Kit with Co2 Gun.

I was tired of cheaping out when buying spray paint and getting stuck with jobs half finished. Also, the color selection is limited in the store, so I made a refillable spray paint can. Essentially it is just two valve stems soldered onto a can, cheap and effective. Because of saftey concerns when compressing flamable liquids with air, I made a co2 injection kit. Then I realized you could pretty much put anything in these cans, water, air, oil, ect. Food products should not be put in non food safe containers. Do not overcharge either. Internet tells me overcharge is past 80 psi but im sure it depends on product. No rusty cans please.
