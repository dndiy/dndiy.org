A multi generational DIY series for educational use only.

Our purpose is to make demonstrational videos of low end sciency things. We use only the most basic materials possible. Each project will be used in successive projects adding layers of complexity until amazing things are produced, or not. Whatever.

Do Not It Yourself is currently a not for profit organization. You do not have permission to use any of these ideas for commercial use. We will not accept liability of anything done with the projects or information provided here.

This project started on 1/25/2014. We hope to provide demostrational videos that will educate, and entertain.

Why do we exist? Our project series designed with the intent of successive projects building on the previous ones, like a house of cards, maybe a pyramid scheme. Our current goal is to grow and develop in quality, presenting a fun time and opportunity to those involved. Its also educational. We encourage people to think outside the box when attempting to achieve goals. Our projects celebrate DIY culture encouraging and enabling others.
