Various Bicycle failures

I thought it may be a good idea to show the hip kids I can be like them, and tried to make a bicycle and ride it around...and then the failure started.


The Swiss Army Bike.

An adjustable wheelbase bike with torsion bar steering. This one had various attachments you cut put on it. Designed to acomidate multiple passengers if needed.


Th Cargo Unicycle.

A one wheeled bike with counter weights on either side for stability, also features multiple gearing speeds.


Bike To Go

A mini Version of the Swiss Army Bike. Fully adjustable, foldable, torsion bar steering.


The Torpedo

This was the first bike I tried to make. It was a recumbent hybrid electric bike. Features included regernerative brakes, tank style/360 degree turning radius , and a 'launch' button. There were two different ways you could ride it - sport or cruiser mode.

Bike Welder

The Tansformer

A folding bike that could become several different bike styles. It could "transform" styles while riding even. Sometimes it would transform without your approval.


The Electric Amish Cargo Scooter

Based off of a cargo bike and an Amish Scoooter, now you can scoot and ride! Designed for large capacities, you can cary all your groceries, and maybe a passenger too. The capacity of a car, but you dont have to wait in traffic! This one isn't finished yet.
